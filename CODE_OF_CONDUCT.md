# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a welcoming and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove,
edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions that violate
this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to: `conduct@arudchayan.dev`

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
